Coin Conundrum is a game that can be enjoyed by anyone. The goal is to get more coins than anyone else. This is a conundrum though, because everyone has the same goal. Getting lucky cards helps you win, but winners often have good memory and strategy. When cards are put back in the deck piles they can be memorized. Players may also use strategy to choose when to end the game. They can select different cards to end the game sooner or later. Coin Conundrum also has an abstract creative theme. It has many things like treasure chests, coins, and wood. This is all created with color and detail. Coin conundrum may even make you think of going on a treasure hunt because of the wonderful art. But at the same time its design is abstract, bold, and bright. As you can see Coin Conundrum is a good coin-trading problem to have.
